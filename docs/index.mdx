<p id="homepage-quote">A curated collection of best practices for software practitioners building greener software.</p>

## Get Started

Patterns are designed to be easy to understand and put into practice. Just follow these simple steps:

<table id="getting-started">
  <tr>
    <th><img src="img/homepage-started.png" /></th>
    <td><strong>Choose a pattern</strong> that fits your role or aligns with where you are in the software development lifecycle.</td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <th><img src="img/homepage-started.png" /></th>
    <td><strong>Review the pattern and its proposed solution</strong> to understand the core idea.</td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <th><img src="img/homepage-started.png" /></th>
    <td><strong>Identify how the pattern applies to your software</strong>, including what changes are needed and how they may impact your system</td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <th><img src="img/homepage-started.png" /></th>
    <td><strong>Apply the change</strong> and measure the impact.</td>
  </tr>
</table>
